WE ARE
AURA

A collective of designers, artisans, and dreamers committed to redefining the relationship between clothing and consciousness.

Fashion is not
about clothing.
It's about identity.

Founded in the heart of Copenhagen, AURA emerged from a simple yet profound realization: the modern wardrobe had lost its soul. In an era of fast fashion and disposable trends, we chose to slow down.

Our journey began in a small atelier with three sewing machines and a vision—to create garments that outlive seasons, that carry stories, and that make the wearer feel unmistakably themselves.

Today, we remain independent, family-owned, and fiercely committed to our founding principles. Every piece that bears the AURA label has passed through the hands of artisans who share our belief that excellence is not an act, but a habit.

OUR JOURNEY

2012

The Beginning

AURA is founded in a converted warehouse in Copenhagen's Meatpacking District. Our first collection consists of 12 handcrafted pieces.

2016

Sustainability First

We commit to 100% organic materials and ethical manufacturing. The launch of our "Zero Waste" initiative reshapes our production process.

2020

Global Recognition

AURA receives the International Sustainable Design Award. We expand to 15 countries while maintaining our local production ethos.

2024

The Future

Launch of our Circular Fashion program. We introduce biodegradable packaging and achieve carbon-negative operations.

OUR VALUES

The principles that guide every decision we make, from fiber to finished garment.

Sustainability

We use only organic, recycled, or regenerative materials. Our supply chain is fully traceable from farm to hanger.

Craftsmanship

Every garment is constructed with precision. We partner with ateliers that have perfected their craft over generations.

Community

We invest in the communities where we operate. Fair wages, safe conditions, and opportunities for growth are non-negotiable.

Transparency

We believe you have the right to know how your clothes are made. Full disclosure on pricing, sourcing, and impact.

THE ATELIER

Our flagship atelier in Copenhagen remains the heart of AURA. Here, master tailors work alongside young designers, blending traditional techniques with innovative sustainable practices.

Visitors are welcome to witness the creation process, meet our artisans, and understand the journey of a garment from concept to completion.
